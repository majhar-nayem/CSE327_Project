1.Introduction

1.1 Purpose

Restaurant digital odering system is a way in which customers can order foods through online in a restaurant.Customer can watch their food and can choose their foods. Through this project customers can get more conveniences. They can see their foods in 3d diagrams and can online order.There are many menus and food catagory.Customers can select foods and can know their foods prices through their android app and web.

1.2 Document Conventions

Food menus are well designed and CSS is used. Foods catagories are in tabular form where customers can see food lists(Bengali,Indian,Chinese....).

1.3 Intended Audience and Reading Suggestions

there are two users Admin and customers. Admin control the whole system. In the begining we design a web page like where will the buttons and how the food lists will display.....Admin Login-> Take Order,Bill Print,Add/ Remove Food Items,Add Offers.User Login->Menu Items,Place an Order,Combo Box Selection,Bill Payment.Then we fix a project perspective and UMl diagram. Then we fix the product functions,user classes and characteristics,External Interface Requirements,system features,other nonfunctional requirements. *Software engineering – Ninth Edition (by Ian Sommerville)

1.4 Product Scope

In the project scope : There is database for different types of foods and some for admin. Admin can modify food names,delete ,add,change price..... Out of project scope: Lisense of server
